# Payments and Transactions

Ignite Tournaments will keep track of every transaction within the system, whether the transaction brings money in or out of the system or moves the money between usersâ€™ balances.

Ignite Tournaments supports the following types of transactions:

* Deposits
* Entry Fees
* Paybacks
* Payouts
* Withdrawals
* Staking

Every user can see the full history of their own transactions. The transaction history is private to that particular user.
